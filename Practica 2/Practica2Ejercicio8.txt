 NUM2 DB ?

ORG 2000h
 MOV BX, OFFSET MSJ
 MOV AL, OFFSET NUM1 - OFFSET MSJ
 INT 7

 INT 6
 
 MOV BX, OFFSET MSJ
 MOV AL, OFFSET NUM1 - OFFSET MSJ
 INT 7
 MOV BX, OFFSET NUM2
 INT 6

 MOV CL,NUM2
